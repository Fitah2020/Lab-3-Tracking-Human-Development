Tracking Human Development Over Time: Fitah Abdirahman

This assignment used data from the United Nations Development Program in order to develop the Human Development Index. The scope of this assignment was to compare composite Indicies over two periods of time. This is so we are able to analyze a neighbourhoods wellbeing or understand changes in demographics. By clicking on a country you are able to see the Gdp estimate, population estimate and the human development index and compare the 2010 hdi scores to the 2018 hdi scores.
